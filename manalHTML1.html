Two Little Cats

Backstory

Once upon a night, I heard little meows underneath my kitchen's balcony, so I checked it out,and there appeared 4 little adorable kittens.One was black, one was blonde and two were grey.

Poor kittens

I kept checking up on them,but after few days, they seemed to be getting weak and hungry. I was sure something happened to their mother and they became on their own. So, I bought a pet nursing bottle an some lactose free milk, and started looking after them.
Unfortunately only 2 kittens survived: Fluffy and Grumpy, meet them.
